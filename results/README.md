\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Results\
\
This folder contains the numerical results produced by the simulation studies and real-data analyses presented in the paper:\
\
**Robust Genomic Prediction and Heritability Estimation using Density Power Divergence**  \
Chowdhury et al., *Crop Science*, 2024  \
https://doi.org/10.1002/csc2.21430\
\
All results reported in the paper are fully reproducible using the scripts provided in the `simulation/` and `methods/` directories.\
\
---\
\
## \uc0\u55357 \u56522  Contents of the Results\
\
The results correspond to **prediction accuracy** and **heritability estimation** under both **one-stage** and **two-stage** linear mixed model frameworks.\
\
They include:\
\
- Prediction accuracy measures:\
  - Pearson correlation coefficient (\uc0\u961 \u770 )\
  - Median Absolute Deviation (MAD)\
- Heritability estimates (\uc0\u292 \u8346 )\
- Mean Square Deviation (MSD) of heritability estimates\
- Comparisons under:\
  - Pure (uncontaminated) data\
  - Random contamination\
  - Block contamination\
\
---\
\
## \uc0\u55358 \u56810  Simulation Results (Artificial Data)\
\
### One-stage models\
Results summarized in **Table 1** of the paper include:\
- RMLA\
- RMLV\
- Robust RMLA (Rob-RMLA)\
- Robust RMLV (Rob-RMLV)\
- Proposed MDPDE-based methods for \uc0\u945  \u8712  \{0.1, 0.3, 0.5, 0.7, 1\}\
\
Metrics reported:\
- Test data correlation (\uc0\u961 \u770 )\
- Test data MAD\
\
### Two-stage models\
Results summarized in **Table 2** include:\
- Rob1\
- Rob2\
- Proposed MDPDE-based methods (\uc0\u945  \u8712  \{0.1, 0.3, 0.5, 0.7, 1\})\
\
The two-stage MDPDE approach consistently achieves:\
- Higher predictive accuracy\
- Lower MAD under contamination\
\
---\
\
## \uc0\u55358 \u56812  Heritability Estimation Results\
\
Heritability estimates are evaluated using:\
- Average estimated heritability\
- Mean Square Deviation (MSD) from the true heritability (H\'b2 = 0.7)\
\
Results are reported in **Table 3**:\
- One-stage procedures\
- Two-stage procedures\
- Performance under pure and contaminated data\
\
The proposed MDPDE-based estimators show:\
- Lower MSD\
- Greater stability under block contamination\
\
---\
\
## \uc0\u55356 \u57149  Real Data Results (Maize Dataset)\
\
Real-data results are obtained from a CIMMYT maize dataset with:\
- 284 genotypes\
- 1135 SNP markers\
- Two environments (drought stress and well-watered)\
\
Reported in **Table 4**, including:\
- Prediction accuracy (\uc0\u961 \u770 , MAD)\
- Estimated heritability (\uc0\u292 \u8346 )\
\
Key observations:\
- One-stage MDPDE (\uc0\u945  = 1) provides the best predictive accuracy\
- Two-stage methods are less effective due to low marker-effect variance\
- Estimated heritability is close to zero, indicating strong environmental influence\
\
